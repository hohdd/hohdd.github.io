  • How are you?

  • It’s nice to meet you
  • I’m pleased to meet you.
  • It’s a pleasure to meet you.

  • How do you do?

General greetings (Informal)

  • Hi
  • Hey
  • What’s up?
  • How’s it going?
  • How have you been?
  • What’s new?
  • What’s going on?
  • How are things?
  • How’s your day?
  • How’s your day going?

Useful responses when greeting people

  • I’m fine, thank you.
  • Great, thanks. How are you?
